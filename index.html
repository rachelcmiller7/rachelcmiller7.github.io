My name is Rachel Miller and I am a Junior Interactive Design Major at James Madison University here in Harrisonburg, Virginia. I have a passion for clean and sleek design as well as music, photography and art. The purpose of this website is for you to get to know all about me!


I was born in Frederick, Maryland and lived in Northern Virginia until I was 7 years old. At that age my dad took a job in the United Kingdom and I've lived there ever since. Family is super important to me, and I try to spend as much time with them as possible whenever I can.


I grew up as a musician, playing piano, violin, flute, trombone and singing. Through this I spent a lot of my high school years touring around Europe singing with different bands and soaking up the culture.


Though music has always held a special place in my heart, design is my passion. I love the way an effective design can completely transform a message, and I hope to develop my skills further to become truely equipt with the expertees to code the ideas that I think up.
